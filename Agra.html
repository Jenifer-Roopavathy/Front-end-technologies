Two-Hour Agra Day Tour

A guided sightseeing city tour

On this two-hour, guided Agra sightseeing tour, you’ll explore the city using battery-operated rickshaws. You’ll begin the tour from the Sheroes Hangout Cafe in Agra. On the morning tour, you’ll observe the city waking up to the sounds of the ‘azan’ morning prayer from nearby mosques and the ringing of temple bells. You’ll also see children heading to school. Additionally, you’ll see the sweet shops preparing local breakfast delicacies such as ‘jalebis’ and ‘kachoris’. You’ll also view attractions such as the Shri Mankameshwar Mandir, Jama Masjid, Akbar Church, Dara Shikoh’s Library, Mirza Ghalib’s birthplace, the headquarters of the Radhasoami faith, churches of various Christian sects and St. Patrick's Junior College, one of Asia’s oldest convents.
