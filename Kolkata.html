Wetlands Walking Tour

A walk amidst nature to explore the wetlands

Instant confirmation

Free cancellation available

On this walking tour, you will explore the wetlands of Kolkata. After driving to one of the entry points of wetlands, you will go on a 2-2.5 hours hike amidst the lush greenery. Along with a guide, you will walk through the trails covering water bodies and village community doing fish farming. There may be a short stop at a local shack for tea and snacks. After the walk, the guide will drop you to the nearest taxi point.
