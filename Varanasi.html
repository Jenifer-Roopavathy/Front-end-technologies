This guided, two-hour tour will introduce you to the rich culture and history of Varanasi with an Aarti ceremony experience, a walking tour of the city’s ghats and a boat ride along the Ganges River. You will start in the morning and watch the first light break over the city, cascading across the Ganges and spilling down buildings and streets. At the same time, you will get to witness a religious ceremony where people in saffron robes wave incense sticks or lamps while chanting or singing hymns. Afterwards, you will cruise along the Ganges River and be treated to views of the vibrant city. You will complete your tour with a stroll through Varanasi’s ghats.
